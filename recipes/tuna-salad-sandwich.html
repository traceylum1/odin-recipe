Tuna Salad Sandwich

Description

If you're a fan of tuna, this tuna salad sandwich is a must-try! With only a few ingredients, you'll be obsessed with this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of tuna
  • 1 hard boiled egg chopped
  • quarter cup of celery chopped finely
  • 5 tbsp of mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 2 tbsp pepper
  • 2 pickles chopped finely
  • quarter cup of white onion chopped finely
  • 2 slices of bread

Steps

  1. Drain tuna and place into mixing bowl
  2. Mix in mayonnaise
  3. Add chopped egg, onion, celery and pickles and mix
  4. Add salt and pepper
  5. Use a spoon and scoop a generous portion of tuna salad onto a slice of bread
  6. Enjoy :)
