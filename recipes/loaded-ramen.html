Loaded Ramen

Description

Instant ramen is a great way to fill your stomach, but most people are looking for more than just carbs in their meal. If you're short on time and you need a quick and satiating meal in less than 15 minutes, try making our loaded instant ramen!

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of ramen
  • 1 egg
  • half cup of chopped kimchi
  • half white onion chopped
  • half cup ground pork
  • 2 cloves of chopped garlic
  • quarter cup of chopped spring onion

Steps

  1. Heat a tablespoon of oil on a pan
  2. Cook the garlic until lightly browned
  3. Add onion and stir fry until translucent
  4. Add ground pork and stir until browned
  5. Add 2 cups of water to the pan and bring to a boil
  6. Add the ramen seasoning and dry ingredients
  7. add dry noodles and boil for 2 minutes
  8. Move everything into serving bowl
  9. Crack an egg in the pan and cook sunny side up
  10. Place the egg on top of the bowl of ramen
  11. Sprinkle chopped spring onion over the ramen
  12. Enjoy :)
