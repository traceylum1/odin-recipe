Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

Kimchi fried rice is a simple but delicious meal you can make in less than 30 minutes. With the perfect amount of protein, veggies, and carbs, this recipe is sure to be a new staple in your cooking repertoire!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups chopped kimchi
  • 1 whole white onion chopped
  • 1 can of spam
  • 5 cloves of chopped garlic
  • half cup of chopped spring onion
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of kimchi water

Steps

  1. Heat a tablespoon of oil on a pan
  2. Cook the garlic until lightly browned
  3. Add onion and stir fry until translucent
  4. Add spam and kimchi, cook until lightly crispy on the outisde
  5. Add rice to the pan and stir
  6. Add soy sauce and kimchi water to the rice while stirring
  7. Plate the rice
  8. Crack two eggs in the pan and cook them sunny side up
  9. Place the eggs on top of the fried rice
  10. Sprinkle chopped spring onion over the rice
  11. Enjoy :)
